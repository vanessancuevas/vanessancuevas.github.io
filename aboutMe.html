{ A designer is a problem solver }

Bay Area native, Vanessa studied for a degree in Psychology (B.S.) before deciding upon her current major, Design: Visual Communications (B.S.) at San Francisco State University. As she entered the world of design, she began to notice the ethics of the user experience were generally founded on "user-centered" designs rather than "human-centered" designs.

She took the foundations she had in Psychology and began to apply it to her research and designs. Ultimately, her goal is to humanize and evolve the visual language of a product.
