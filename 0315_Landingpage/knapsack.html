VANESSANCUEVAS.design
The only social travel app you'll need


Project Goals

To create a platform that encourages travel by establishing a centralized location for destination searches, communication amongst companions, and recommended items to pack based on their travel destination.

Timeline

October 12, 2020 - November 2, 2020

Research Method

Pain points were identified by surveying students at San Francisco State University.

Deliverable

A mobile app prototype for iOS in Adobe Xd

What's wrong with current travel discovery?

“I feel like I still go to the same places and never know what to pack. I always forget something and have to buy it on my trip.” - SFSU Student

  • Users are unsure of what to pack
  • They have difficulty coordinating schedules
  • As a group they are unable to quickly make decisions
  • They have to search on several different platforms for important accommodation details while communicating on other platforms.

How does Knapsack improve travel discovery?

No, you don’t have to upload your whole house into lists

  • Use Knapsack as you pack from now on.

  • Save your lists and Knapsack will remind you what you pack for similar destinations and trips.

  • Over time, Knapsack will recommend destinations to travel based on your items, activities, and companionships.

We have different needs at different times of the year.

As users search for destinations, a photo of the destination in its current season gives the user a sense of the weather conditions. Seasonal recommendations are offered based on average annual weather conditions (future feature: user reported tips).

Compare how many of your Companions have also traveled to the same destinations

Our Calendars feature allows you to sync iCal or Google Calendar to find recommended destinations based on your location and the time of year.

Alter your priorities ($$$, distance from home, activities offered) to adjust your recommendations at any time. Recommended lists based on destination activities.

Search, share, and chat in one app

Share destinations, create lists, and vote on group decisions all in one place without switching from app to app.

Getting everyone to agree is hard. That’s why we implemented group votes and item requests to help users make these decisions quickly.

Want to go your separate ways on a trip?

Share your location in the chat so your companions can see the relative distance you are from them.
ex. Rebecca is 5 mi from your location
